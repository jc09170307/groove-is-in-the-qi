Clinical notes
Korean lunar medicine 한의학
In classical Korean medicine (한의학), the lunar cycle governs the waxing and waning of Wei Qi (defensive energy) and Blood. Practitioners historically timed tonification on the waxing moon and sedation on the waning moon. The full moon amplifies Yang — sleep can be lighter, emotions more intense. The new moon draws energy inward — ideal for rest, reflection, and consolidating Jing.
